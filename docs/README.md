# OctoAcme Project Management Docs

This README provides a high-level summary of OctoAcme's project management processes and quick links to the detailed process documents stored in the docs/ folder.

## Project Management Overview

OctoAcme follows an **iterative, customer-focused project management approach** designed to deliver value efficiently while maintaining quality and team alignment. Our processes are structured around clear roles, lightweight but effective artifacts, and a regular cadence of activities.

### Key Principles

- **Iterative Delivery**: We break work into small, manageable increments with frequent feedback loops
- **Clear Roles & Accountability**: Product Manager (PM), Product Designer (PdM), Developers, and QA have well-defined responsibilities
- **Lightweight Documentation**: We use minimal but essential artifacts to guide and track work
- **Regular Cadence**: Standups, weekly syncs, demos, and retrospectives keep the team aligned and continuously improving
- **Customer-Focused**: Every decision is evaluated for customer impact and value delivery

### Core Activities by Phase

**Initiation**: Projects begin with a One-pager and approvals process to ensure alignment on goals, scope, and success criteria.

**Planning**: Detailed planning includes backlog creation, story estimation, and Definition of Done (DoD) to establish clear expectations.

**Execution & Tracking**: We maintain momentum through daily standups, tracking progress against the backlog, and ensuring small, reviewable PRs that enable CI/CD practices.

**Release & Deployment**: Controlled releases follow documented checklists, include rollback plans, and are communicated clearly to stakeholders.

**Continuous Improvement**: Retrospectives at the end of each cycle capture lessons learned and drive action items for process improvements.
